== Description ==

Sedona Shop is a modern WordPress theme for furniture and interior design stores powered by WooCommerce and Elementor page builder.
With its advanced customizer options, you can easily customize the theme to match your brand's unique style and create a truly captivating online shopping experience for your customers.

This theme is WooCommerce ready, which means you can effortlessly set up and manage your online store, showcase your furniture and interior decor products, and handle transactions securely and efficiently. Whether you're selling sofas, DIY, decor, tables, lighting fixtures, or home accessories, Sedona Shop has got you covered.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
